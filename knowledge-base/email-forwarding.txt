Date: 2026-03-21

Problem
-------
Needed a professional contact email (contact@cadarena.dev) for the CAD Arena project
without paying for a full email hosting service.

Solution
--------
ImprovMX email forwarding — free tier, no SMTP needed.
All mail sent to contact@cadarena.dev forwards to maksym.d.bondarenko@gmail.com.

Steps Taken
-----------
1. Domain cadarena.dev was already registered via Vercel (expires Mar 8, 2027).
   DNS is managed by Vercel nameservers (ns1.vercel-dns.com / ns2.vercel-dns.com).

2. Signed up at improvmx.com, added cadarena.dev as a domain, and created the alias:
     contact@cadarena.dev → maksym.d.bondarenko@gmail.com

3. Followed ImprovMX's Generic DNS Configuration guide:
   https://improvmx.com/guides/generic-dns-configuration

   Added the following records in Vercel's DNS panel (Vercel dashboard → cadarena.dev → DNS Records):
     Name: @   Type: MX    Value: mx1.improvmx.com          Priority: 10
     Name: @   Type: MX    Value: mx2.improvmx.com          Priority: 20
     Name: @   Type: TXT   Value: v=spf1 include:spf.improvmx.com ~all

4. Clicked "Check again" in ImprovMX — forwarding activated once DNS propagated.

Notes
-----
- ImprovMX free plan supports unlimited aliases and unlimited forwarding.
- No Gmail confirmation required to receive forwarded mail.
- To send FROM contact@cadarena.dev (not just receive), would need ImprovMX Premium ($9/mo)
  or configure Gmail "Send mail as" with an SMTP provider.
- Vercel auto-renews the domain at $13/yr.
