## Objective
Conduct an extensive search for all available research and industry activity at the intersection of **AI and CAD / mechanical engineering**. Two deliverables:

1. **Research papers** — downloaded as PDFs + a structured database (`papers-database.md`)
2. **Companies & startups** — a comprehensive list (`companies.md`)

- [ ] 5. Write up the companies list with descriptions

---

## Scope of Interest

### Core topics (highest priority)
- AI/ML generating CAD models (3D solid models, parametric CAD)
- Text-to-CAD / natural language to CAD
- Image-to-CAD / sketch-to-CAD
- LLMs for mechanical design / engineering design automation
- AI-driven parametric modeling (OpenSCAD, STEP, BREP generation)
- Generative design (topology optimization with AI)
- AI for CAD code generation (OpenSCAD, CadQuery, FreeCAD scripting)

### Adjacent topics (include if relevant)
- AI for 3D shape generation (if tied to engineering/CAD, not just artistic 3D)
- Point cloud / scan to CAD reconstruction
- AI for FEA / simulation-driven design
- AI for manufacturing (DFM, DFA with AI assistance)
- Foundation models for 3D / geometry
- Multimodal models applied to engineering drawings
- Inverse CAD / CAD program synthesis from shapes
- AI for assembly design and constraint solving
