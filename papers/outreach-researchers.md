## Outreach Templates

### Cold email — faculty mentor ask
Subject: Benchmark for AI in mechanical engineering — seeking feedback

> Hi Prof. [X],
> I'm Maksym Bondarenko, an independent researcher working on two projects at the intersection of AI and mechanical engineering. I've built CAD Arena (cadarena.dev), a live benchmark comparing text-to-CAD models, and I'm developing a Lego Technic benchmark around the thesis that mechanical feedback interpretation is the key bottleneck preventing AI from being useful in real ME tasks.
>
> Your work on [specific paper] is directly relevant. I'd love to get 20 minutes of your time to get feedback on the direction — and potentially discuss collaboration or co-authorship if there's interest.
>
> Happy to share a draft outline.

### Researcher collaboration ask
Subject: CAD Arena — would you be interested in benchmarking [their model]?

> Hi [Name],
> I'm running CAD Arena (cadarena.dev), a live benchmark for text-to-CAD models. Your work on [X] seems like a natural fit. I'd love to include your model in the benchmark — you'd get visibility, a public leaderboard entry, and an independent comparison against commercial and academic baselines.
>
> Would you be open to a quick call?
